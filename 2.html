AE

Neeko is offered a selection of Champion icon allied champions to take their appearance. Once selected, there's a 2.5 seconds delay before another champion can be selected.
While disguised, Neeko can use basic attacks while keeping her form. Taking damage from enemy champions, or casting either Blooming Burst Blooming Burst or Tangle-Barbs Tangle-Barbs breaks the illusion and puts Inherent Glamour on Cooldown reduction icon cooldown.
When disguised, Neeko can select herself to deactivate her disguise, putting Inherent Glamour on a 2-second Cooldown reduction icon cooldown.
